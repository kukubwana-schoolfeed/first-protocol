Our Story

WHO WE ARE

First Protocol Logistics was founded with a singular purpose: to make trade across Zambia's borders simpler, faster, and fully compliant. We saw too many businesses losing time and money to clearance delays, incorrect documentation, and a lack of real communication from their clearing agents.

We built First Protocol around the opposite of that experience — meticulous documentation, direct ZRA relationships, and a client-first service model that treats every shipment as if it were our own. From a small operation clearing trucks at Chirundu, we have grown into a full-service logistics company serving importers, exporters, and traders across the SADC and COMESA region.

Today, we operate across all major Zambian border posts — Kazungula, Chirundu, Nakonde — with offices in Lusaka, Ndola, Kitwe, and Livingstone. Our team combines decades of customs experience with modern systems that give clients real-time visibility into every stage of their clearance.

Our Direction

MISSION & VISION

Our Mission MISSION

To provide businesses with seamless, compliant, and transparent customs clearing and freight forwarding services that reduce the cost and complexity of trade across Zambia and the SADC region — delivered by experts who treat every client's cargo as their own priority.

Our Vision VISION

To be the most trusted logistics partner for businesses trading into and out of Zambia — recognized across the SADC and COMESA region for our expertise, integrity, and technology-driven approach to trade facilitation. We aim to set the standard for what a clearing agent can and should be.

What Drives Us

OUR CORE VALUES

Four principles that shape every interaction, every clearance, and every decision we make.

Integrity

We declare accurately, advise honestly, and never cut corners — even when it would be easier. Our clients trust us with their compliance and their reputation.

Efficiency

Time at the border costs money. We streamline every step — documentation preparation, ZRA submission, physical inspection coordination — to minimize dwell time.

Innovation

We embrace technology — from digital documentation management to real-time client tracking — to deliver a modern experience in an industry often resistant to change.

Client-First

Every client gets a dedicated account manager. Every question is answered. Every update is communicated proactively. Your success is our only measure of performance.
