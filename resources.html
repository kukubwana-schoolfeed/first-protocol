ZRA Introduces Updated e-Declaration System

The Zambia Revenue Authority has rolled out an updated version of its ASYCUDA system for customs declarations, introducing new fields for COMESA certificate of origin data and improved duty calculation transparency.

SADC Tariff Liberalization Schedule Update

New tariff phase-down rates have come into effect for SADC member states under the 2026 liberalization schedule. Several categories including textiles, processed foods, and electronics have seen further duty reductions.

Kazungula Bridge — New Lane Operations Expand

The Kazungula Bridge authority has expanded dedicated lanes for commercial vehicles, significantly reducing crossing wait times. Average truck crossing time has decreased from 6–8 hours to 2–4 hours under the new operating procedures.
